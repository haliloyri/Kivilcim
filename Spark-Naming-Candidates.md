# Spark — Name Candidates

A daily wisdom & inspiration app: one short "spark" of insight per day, distilled from books. Primary launch markets **Germany** and **Spain**; must also work in English and Turkish. Tone: warm, premium, calm, intelligent, modern. Every name below is built from sounds that read **cleanly and almost identically in German and Spanish** (vowels a/e/i/o/u plus l, m, n, r, p, b, t, d, f, s, k) and avoids the traps: English "th/w/soft-j", Spanish-divergent letters (z, c+e/i, ll, qu, v, j), and the awkward initial "sp-" cluster.

> **A note on the working name "Spark":** it's a lovely concept, but the word itself is the weakest performer in exactly your two priority markets. In German, *Spark-* immediately evokes *sparen / Sparkasse* (saving / savings bank); in Spanish, an initial *sp-* is hard to pronounce and gets vocalized as *"es-park."* Keep "Spark" as the English/metaphor anchor — but the names below are designed to feel premium and native to a German and Spanish ear.

## Top 3 recommendations

### 1. Faro
The single best concept-to-name fit: a **lighthouse** is a calm, steady, guiding light — exactly the feeling of one daily spark that helps you find your way. It reads *FAH-ro* identically in German and Spanish, is short, warm and premium, and carries a positive meaning in Spanish (lighthouse) while staying clean and easy in German. The metaphor extends naturally into design (a beam, a horizon) and copy ("today's light").

- **Tagline:** *Your daily guiding light.*
- **App Store title (26):** `Faro: Daily Wisdom & Spark`
- **German + Spanish:** ✅ Clean and natural in **both**. ES = "lighthouse" (positive); DE = no meaning, effortless to say. No negative or vulgar sense in either. Note: secure `faro.app`/`getfaro` and check the FARO Technologies mark (different category).

### 2. Lumira
An **invented** name — so the cleanest path to a matching .com/.app and App Store title — that still *means* something: it fuses *lumen* (light) with *mira* (look). It sounds expensive and feminine-soft, reads *loo-MEE-ra* identically in both markets, and looks elegant on an icon and as a hashtag. The safest pick for actually owning the brand outright.

- **Tagline:** *A little light, every day.*
- **App Store title (28):** `Lumira: Daily Ideas & Wisdom`
- **German + Spanish:** ✅ Clean and natural in **both**. No dictionary meaning, no negative or vulgar sense in DE or ES; reads premium to both ears. Note: SAP has a product called "Lumira" (different category) — verify trademark class.

### 3. Estela
The original, premium sleeper: Spanish for the luminous **trail a comet leaves** — a beautiful image of insight that lingers. It reads *es-TE-la* identically in both languages, sounds Mediterranean-premium to a German ear, and is far less brand-crowded than the light/dawn words. Poetic without being soft.

- **Tagline:** *A trail of daily insight.*
- **App Store title (24):** `Estela: Your Daily Spark`
- **German + Spanish:** ✅ Clean and natural in **both**. ES = "trail/wake of light" (positive, poetic) and a familiar first name; DE = no meaning, easy and premium to pronounce. No negative or vulgar sense in either.
